About Me

My name is Hans Aguilar. I am 16 years old, born on the 4th of April in 2005. I have been studying programming for three years, web development for two years, and networking for two years. I have lived in Canada for four years. While I have experience in networking, my skills in software and web development are more refined. I focus on software development and web development because I find those more interesting and I find cybersecurity to be too big a challenge. The possibilities are nearly endless when programming, with all kinds of options for tools for the job, but you do not need to remember everything, just the basics. The same goes for HTML and CSS. Cybersecurity, on the other hand, is more focused and requires more memorization. As my memory is lackluster, software development fits my purposes for that reason.
